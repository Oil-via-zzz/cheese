📝 How to Play

  • Use WASD to move and mouse to look.
  • Press Q to punch or mine when equipped.
  • Press E to pick up nearby items.
  • Press 1-4 to select a hotbar slot and Q to equip it.
  • Press C to open crafting, then craft tools like axe, gun, and pickaxe.
  • Use Sell and Trade to earn coins and buy upgrades.
